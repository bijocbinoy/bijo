ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Bijo C Binoy, a recent graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Applications specializing in Big Data Analytics and Cloud Computing from Yenepoya University, class of 2024. My academic journey has equipped me with a solid foundation in technology, and my hands-on experiences have honed my skills in various domains.

During my time at Yenepoya University, I was not only an active student but also deeply involved in extracurricular activities. I served as the secretary of the IT Club, where I organized several programs, including gaming events and basic web development workshops for fellow students. Additionally, I was appointed as the president of the YENOVA Club, where I played a key role in organizing the IBM ICE Day, featuring a 24-hour hackathon and other exciting events.

I also completed an internship in Python development at Inmakes Infotech in Kochi, India. This experience allowed me to apply my knowledge in real-world scenarios, further enhancing my skills in web development using Flask and Django, as well as data analytics and machine learning.

My leadership roles in college, including my membership in the student council, have taught me valuable lessons in collaboration and guidance. I believe in giving people the freedom to work independently and providing constructive feedback to help them grow.

Outside of my professional life, I have a keen interest in agriculture and animal husbandry. Engaging in agriculture and caring for animals has taught me patience, responsibility, and the importance of sustainable practices. I am driven by a commitment to excellence and a passion for innovation. Looking ahead, I am excited about the opportunities to contribute to impactful projects and collaborate with like-minded professionals in the tech industry.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. Please feel free to explore my work and reach out if you'd like to connect or collaborate.
